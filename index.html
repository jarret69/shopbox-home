Q: How does ShopBox work?
A: In 3 simple steps. Shop. Click. Collect.
1. Shop. Search for your item on our site or copy and paste the link from any of our partner stores to add your item then order your products where you can check out multiple items from one or more stores in one cart.
2. Click. Pay one all-inclusive cost using your credit or Visa Debit card or cash at one of our Pay Point locations
3. Relax. Your items will be delivered to your home or one of our pick-up locations.
